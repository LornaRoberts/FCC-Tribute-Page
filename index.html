Benedict Cumberbatch

Award Winning British Actor and Producer

Benedict Carlton Cumberbatch was born in 1976 in London, UK. His parents, Timothy Carlton and Wanda Ventham are both actors. He had one older half-sister, Tracy, who died in 2020.

Benedict went to boarding school at a young age, first to Brambletyre Prep School in Hampshire where he was on the cricket and rugby teams then onto Harrow School in North West London on an arts scholarship. While he was there, he was also a member of the National Youth Theatre.

After school, Benedict took a gap year teaching English to Buddhist monks in India. He then returned to England and studied for a BA in drama at the University of Manchester. He followed this up with professional acting training at LAMBDA where he was awarded an MA in Acting.
